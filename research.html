ALBATROS

In late 2022 and early 2023, I had the opportunity to work with a fantastic group at McGill working on the ALBATROS (Array of Long Baseline Antennas for Taking Radio Observations from the Sub-antarctic/Seventy-ninth parallel) under professor Cynthia Chiang. I even wrote a short paper about what I did there. In all honesty, I regret not being able to help them more, but unfortunately I'm no longer in Montreal (for now, at least).
